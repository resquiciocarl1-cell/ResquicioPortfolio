Our Legacy

More Than Just Pares

Paresalamat was born from a simple dream in 2020 — to bring the true soul of traditional Filipino beef pares to the bustling streets of Valenzuela. We set out to create a welcoming place built on honesty, unparalleled quality, and highly affordable prices that absolutely everyone in the community can enjoy, from hardworking students to busy professionals craving a hearty late-night comfort meal.

Every single bowl we serve carries decades of rich culinary tradition. We believe that truly great food takes time. That is why our premium beef brisket is meticulously slow-cooked for over 12 hours, simmering gently in an incredibly aromatic broth infused with star anise, roasted garlic, and our closely guarded secret family spice blend. It is served steaming hot with our signature sinangag (fried garlic rice) and that unforgettable sweet-savory glaze that keeps our loyal customers coming back day after day.

From our humble, passionate beginnings in a small corner at the Arrhelyn Building on Maysan Road, Paresalamat has rapidly grown into a beloved local landmark for anyone craving authentic, comforting Filipino food. We treat every customer like family, ensuring that every bite feels exactly like home. Whether you are looking for a quick budget-friendly bite or a legendary overload feast, you will always find a warm welcome waiting for you here.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your favorite pares.

Do you offer delivery services outside of Valenzuela City?

Currently, our direct hot delivery focuses exclusively on Valenzuela City and immediately surrounding areas to ensure your pares arrives piping hot, fresh, and ready to eat. However, bulk orders and specialized catering requests for outer regions can absolutely be arranged with prior notice. Just send us a message!

What exactly makes your legendary beef pares different from others?

It all comes down to our meticulous slow-cooking process and our highly guarded secret family spice blend. We carefully simmer our premium beef cuts for over 12 hours until they literally melt in your mouth. We absolutely never use cheap shortcuts or artificial thickeners—just pure, hearty, incredibly traditional flavor in every single bite.

Can I easily order in advance for large gatherings, events, or office parties?

Absolutely! We absolutely love feeding a hungry crowd. For large orders of 10 bowls or more, we highly recommend messaging us at least 24 hours in advance via our official Facebook page or WhatsApp number. We will prepare everything perfectly portioned and securely packed for your special event.

Are your meat and fresh ingredients sourced locally?

Yes, 100%! We are incredibly proud to support our hard-working local Valenzuela markets and farmers. Our premium beef, farm-fresh vegetables, and aromatic spices are sourced daily from highly trusted local vendors to guarantee maximum freshness, superior quality, and to continuously support the community that built us.
